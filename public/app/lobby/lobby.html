Gameplay
The goal of Society is to outwit you opponent in a Rock-Paper-Scissors like fashion. When hovering over your options on the bottom side of the board, your opponent's options will either become transparent, highlight green, or highlight red.
-Transparent: Your hovered choice ties with the transparent choice.
-Green: Your hovered choice WINS against the highlighted green choices.
-Red: Your hovered choice LOSES against the highlighted red choices.
Pro-Tip: Consider taking risks by playing options that you have with low health. Your opponent might not see it coming.
